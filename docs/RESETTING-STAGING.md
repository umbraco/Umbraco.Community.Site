# Resetting the Staging Site

If you're restoring content from Production to Staging, after doing the restore you need to:

- Change the name of the top-level nodes (prefix with "Staging ") to help know which environment we're in! 

- Forms section - go to each form and remove any workflow that emails someone, or posts to Zapier. If people want to test that they can put it in themselves and then it's their responsibility to not leave it there!

Of course if people are working on Staging to actually prep content for _pushing_ to live then these rules may not apply, but think it's the safest approach to take until that's the situation!
